What We Do

At Cold Hands Collective, we are dedicated to organizing and hosting Smash Bros. and competitive events for the Calgary community. Whether it's casual tournaments or professional events, we aim to provide high-quality experiences for both players and spectators alike. Join us as we continue to grow and build the scene in Calgary!
