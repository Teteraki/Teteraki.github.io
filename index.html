What We Do

At Cold Hands Collective, we are dedicated to organizing and hosting Smash Bros. and competitive events for the Calgary community. Our goal is to create a welcoming and exciting environment where those of all skill levels can come together, compete, and have fun. Whether it's casual tournaments or professional events, we aim to provide high-quality experiences for both players and spectators alike. Join us as we continue to grow and build the scene in Calgary!
